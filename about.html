--- layout: null section-type: about title: About --- ## About

SpartanUI is designed to free your screen by moving a majority of the interface elements to the bottom. Over the last few years, SpartanUI has transformed to become modular to give you even more control and options over your UI.

Modules

  • Artwork (Bar layouts are managed by the Artwork Style you select)
  • Player Frames
  • Party Frames
  • Raid Frames
  • Spin Cam
  • Film Effects
  • Auto Sell
  • Auto turn in
  • Buffs
  • Minimap
  • Objectives tracker
  • Tooltips

Required Addons

  • Bartender4
